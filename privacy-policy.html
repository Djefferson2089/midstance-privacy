Privacy Policy

Midstance  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2026

Short version: Midstance stores your data on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. The only network request the app ever makes is an anonymous barcode lookup when you scan a food item.

1. Who we are

Midstance is a running cadence and nutrition-tracking app developed by Daniel Jefferson. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at jefferson.daniel2089@gmail.com.

2. Data stored on your device

All app data is saved in your device's local storage and never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it. This includes:

  • Food diary entries (meals, calories, macros)
  • Run history (duration, distance, cadence, calories burned)
  • Body metrics you enter (height, weight, age, sex) — used only to estimate your calorie target
  • App preferences (theme, sound, haptics, BPM presets)
  • Cadence calibration result

None of this information is transmitted to us or any third party. It exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Camera access

Midstance requests camera permission solely to let you scan food barcodes. The camera feed is processed entirely on your device. No images or video are stored or transmitted anywhere.

4. Barcode lookups (Open Food Facts)

When you scan a barcode, the app sends the barcode number to the Open Food Facts public API to retrieve nutritional information. This is the only network request Midstance makes.

The request contains only the barcode number — no name, location, account, or device identifier. Open Food Facts is an open, non-profit food database. You can review their privacy policy at world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy.

5. No analytics or tracking

Midstance contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, advertising networks, or any other third-party code that phones home. We have no way to see how you use the app.

6. No account required

Midstance does not require you to create an account or provide an email address. There is no sign-in and no profile synced to a server.

7. Children's privacy

Midstance is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because all data stays on-device and no personal information is transmitted to us, this app does not collect data from anyone, regardless of age.

8. Your rights and data deletion

Because your data never leaves your device, you are always in full control. To delete all data stored by Midstance, simply uninstall the app. You can also clear individual diary entries or run history from within the app.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Email us at jefferson.daniel2089@gmail.com.
